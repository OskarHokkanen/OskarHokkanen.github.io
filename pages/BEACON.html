26 Aug 2024 - 27 Oct 2024

A tangible interactive prototype created in the course Media Technology and Interaction Design with the goal of bringing inexperienced and experienced gym-goers together. Through a double diamond design process a target group was discovered. Interviews were analysed and a product brought to life. Skills in laser cutting, 3D modeling and printing, graphic design, software development and electronics were necessary in order to complete the hi-fi prototype.
